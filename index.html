Legacy of Hyderabadi Biriyani

Why is Hyderabadi's Dum Biriyani is a famous dish across the world?

Hyderabadi biryani's history is deeply intertwined with the Nizams of Hyderabad, who ruled from the 18th century . The dish, believed to have originated in the royal kitchens, is a fusion of Persian, Mughlai, and local Telugu culinary influences. It evolved into a regal dish, known for its aromatic spices, saffron, and layered cooking method. (Source: Deccan Chronicle)
